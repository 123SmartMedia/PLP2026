# Project Brief: Play Like a Pro Website Redesign

## Overview
Redesign and rebuild the official website for Play Like a Pro.
**Current URL:** https://playlikeaprobaseball.com
**Location:** Hauppauge, NY

## Offerings
1. **Lessons:** Baseball & Softball (Individual and Group)
   - Hitting
   - Pitching
   - Fielding
2. **Clinics:** Group training sessions
3. **Facility Rentals:**
   - Batting Cage Rental
   - Indoor Turf Field Rental (Full or Half)

## Key Features & Requirements
1. **Coach Profiles:** Dedicated pages for instructor bios.
2. **Booking System:** 
   - Lesson scheduler for specific coaches.
   - Facility reservation system (time-based).
3. **Social Proof:** Client testimonials section.
4. **Automation:** Integrated Email & SMS for reminders, confirmations, and marketing.
5. **Analytics:** Website visitor tracking.
6. **Support:** AI Chatbot integration.
7. **Social:** Social media integration links.

## Content Data: Coach Bios
### Chris Scura
**Role:** Lead Hitting Instructor
**Location:** Hauppauge, NY

**Bio:**
Coach Chris Scura is the lead hitting instructor at Play Like a Pro in Hauppauge, NY, where he works with baseball and softball players of all ages to develop consistent, game-ready swings. A veteran instructor at the facility for over a decade, Chris has become a trusted resource for Long Island athletes looking to elevate their offensive game and unlock their full potential.

Drawing on his professional playing background in multiple independent leagues and his experience at respected college programs, Chris blends real-game insight with a modern, detail-oriented approach to hitting. He focuses on building efficient swing mechanics, improving pitch recognition, and developing an approach at the plate that translates under pressure.

In each lesson, Chris uses a clear, positive, and player-first teaching style, making adjustments that match the athlete's age, ability level, and long-term goals. Whether he is working one-on-one or running small-group sessions, his sessions emphasize hard work, accountability, and confidence in the batter's box.

From youth players learning proper fundamentals to high school and college athletes preparing for the next level, Chris is committed to helping every hitter walk away better than when they arrived. His track record of long-term athlete development and his deep roots at Play Like a Pro make him a cornerstone of the training staff in Hauppauge.

## Technical Stack Requirements
- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router)
- **Language:** TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui
- **Backend/Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage, Edge Functions)
- **Payments:** Stripe
- **Hosting:** Vercel
- **Email/SMS:** Resend or Twilio (via Supabase Edge Functions)
